Ferrari

Ferrari is one of the most iconic and prestigious luxury sports car manufacturers in the world, renowned for its high-performance vehicles and rich history in motorsports. Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939, the brand has become synonymous with speed, style, and cutting-edge automotive engineering. Ferrari's signature red cars, known as "Rosso Corsa," are celebrated for their sleek designs and powerful engines, often associated with Formula 1 racing. Over the years, Ferrari has built a reputation for producing not only high-performance machines but also some of the most coveted and collectible cars globally. Each model embodies the brand's legacy of passion, innovation, and excellence, appealing to enthusiasts and collectors alike.
